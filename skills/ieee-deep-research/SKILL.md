# IEEE Deep Research

This is a scaffold skill for IEEE-oriented research support.

## Target behavior

Use this skill to prepare research material for IEEE manuscripts:

- Formulate technical research questions
- Build related-work matrices
- Identify standard baselines and datasets
- Compare methods, assumptions, and evaluation metrics
- Verify claims and source quality
- Prepare evidence maps for IEEE paper sections

## IEEE defaults

- Prioritize peer-reviewed venues, IEEE/ACM/Springer/Elsevier where relevant, and high-quality arXiv only when appropriate.
- Track evaluation datasets, metrics, baselines, code availability, and reproducibility signals.
- Produce outputs that can feed directly into `ieee-academic-paper`.
